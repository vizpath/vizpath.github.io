What is VizPath?

VizPath is an online analytic solution that allows you to
gain insights into your data visually leveraging various types
of data visualizations as your guide.

  • Visual Analytics
  • Custom Dashboards
  • Any Data
  • Teams & Sharing

Visual Analytics

Explore and analyze your data visually.

Using the latest trends and technologies in data visualization, VizPath allows you to dive deeper into your data to gain insights in ways not possible using traditional spreadsheets.

Custom Dashboards

Configure and save your custom dashboards

VizPath gives you the power to save any number of custom dashboards and configurations so you that you can quickly and easily jump back into your exploration later. Save layouts, configurations and filters so that you can clearly tell your story.

Any Data

Use any data you want. From spreadsheets to databases.

VizPath is flexible so it can handle any type of structured data you throw at it. We are continuously working on improving our data connectors to popular services and databases. Let us work with you to get VizPath linked to your system.

Teams & Sharing

Share your projects, data and custom dashboards with anyone

VizPath allows you to share your custom dashboards and findings with other users so that you can collaborate in real-time to gain insights.

Cloud friendly & scalable

Got big data problems? No problem.
Since VizPath is cloud native, you can load in any size data you want.
