What is VizPath?

VizPath is an online analysis solution that allows you to
gain insights into your data visually leveraging various types
of data visualizations as your guide.

  • Load Your Data
  • Choose a Visualization
  • Explore & Tune
  • Export

1. First, insert your data

As simple as a copy-paste.

VizPath works with delimiter-separated values (like .csv and .xlsx files) as well as with copied-and-pasted content from other applications (like Microsoft Excel, Google Spreadsheets, etc).


Don't worry, your data is safe.

VizPath is a web app but the data you load will be processed only by the web browser. No server-side operations or storages are performed, no one will see, touch or copy your data!

2. Choose a visualization

Scatter plots, box plots, and more.

Select from a variety of data visualizations both conventional and unconventional including scatter plot, box plot, dendogram, hexagonal binning, and more. Don’t worry, you can still use bar charts and pies charts too!

3. Explore your data

Find insights and tune your chart

Explore hidden patterns and trends by visually representing your data. Just select the columns in your dataset.

Instant visual feedback

As soon as your columns are mapped you’ll see the visualization appear. Not happy with the result? Keep exploring by changing your selected column mappings and the visualization will update instantly.

4. Export your visualization

Generate & export visuals

VizPath enables you to export visualizations as vector (.svg) or raster (.png) images to use in websites, documents, powerpoints or wherever want to insert your visual.
